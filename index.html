About me

Hello All! My name is Irina, I come from Romania, I moved to the UK few years ago. In January I decided to take the first step in Tech world so I found out about one course - Azure Fundamentals-. I learned a lot of new things about cloud,things that made me want to discover more information related to the cloud architecture, but at that point I wasn't sure which path to choose so my tutor advised me to look for Boot Camp. A friend told me about Code your Future and I decided to look for more information. After doing the Intro Digital Course an idea came across that I should develop these new skills into something more.For me a career in Tech would mean more progression opportunities in my life because the tech industry is evolving and advancing, there is no day without learning something new, so my purpose is to become a full stack developer.

Music

Music is a part of all of our lives, from when we’re growing up to when we’re old. We grow up to the sound of our mothers singing us lullabies so that we’d go to sleep. Music is one of the most calming and soothing things if you let it be. It comes from melodies and tunes strung together by people who sing and play musical instruments.

When we talk about music, we are talking about all the instruments like drums, piano, guitar, etc. and how they get together to make a sound that is pleasing to our ears. We all have some songs we love to sing and dance to, right? Wouldn’t life be a little boring without music? It is something we all need to enjoy our days slightly more than we already do because music adds more color to our lives.
